Our Story

A thousand years of flavor, one table at a time.

Born in Addis Ababa and rooted in the traditions of family gatherings, Buna House brings the warmth of Ethiopian hospitality to the modern city. Our chefs honor the recipes of our grandmothers — slow-cooked, spice-layered, served with love on freshly made injera.

  • ✓ Family recipes from the highlands
  • ✓ Ethically sourced coffee from Yirgacheffe
  • ✓ Hand-pulled injera, made daily
  • ✓ Vegetarian & vegan friendly
